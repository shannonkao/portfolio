A Longitudinal Study of Deadwood Function in Lowland Forest Ecosystems
2024
paper, chalk pastel, ink, digital print
A cylindrical book constructed of textured paper with chalk and oil pastel highlights. Each is constructed by hand and features a set of small papercraft oyster mushrooms. The interior of the book is a scroll of paper measuring 2.5" x 30", and depicts a timelapse of the log.
ebb and rot
2024
4-color risograph
A pair of accordion zines created after a series of trips to the Cascades and the Olympic coast. Risograph printed and featuring macro illustrations of the local ecosystem in flux.
sky/sea
2023
block-printed paper, twine
A book constructed from paper that has been block-printed blue on one side with alternating spreads of blue and white. Each image is a stencil cutout using only the color of the page behind it, with the illustrations carefully designed to be non-overlapping.
Mixtape Zine
2023
plastic cassette case, digital print
A collaborative project between fifteen artists. Each artist designed a 'mixtape' (small playlist) and a 4" x 2.75" accordion zine with each song represented in one page. The final book is a collection of all fifteen zines packaged in two clear cassette boxes. Run of 16.
dirt.net
2023
website
A tiny browser game about poking around in dirt and discovering small wonderful things.
Desire and Fascinate
2022-2023
digital print
A pair of staple-bound books written in a pesudo-academic style about the phenomena of desire paths and fasciation in plants. Full text available online.
shannon kao
Shannon is a Seattle-based artist interested in exploring the intersection of natural and built environments through multidisciplinary practice, with a focus on book-making, print, and digital interfaces. They co-operate the small risograph press snack break studio.
